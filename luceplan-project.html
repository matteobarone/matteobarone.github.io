Brief

The project goal was to create a new visual communication for Luceplan, italian and high quality brand of lights fornitures.

Our concept was based on the details, and why these subtle particular can give a step more for Luceplan to make it stands out against the competitors.

After analysis and brainstorming, we desiged some pages, with a particular attention on the: homepage, product list page and product detail page.

The prototype was delevoped in HTML, CSS and Javascript, and is responsive.

Output

The output was a prototype of the new Luceplan website, focusing on the visual language, very subtle, clean and delicate, based on our concept. We also tried to introduce a more human aspect, playing with the light and trying to give an exclusive experience for the users.

Design for everyone

Our concept wanted to be focused on both Luceplan users: the private clients and the professional clients.

Experience the light

“Experience the Light” is a tool that we developed, on the homepage, to suggest products based on the type of light (spot-light or wide-light).

The user, moving the mouse in the Y-axis (from left to right) on the screen, can see a spot change the shape (left small spot, right wide spot). According to how is the spot in a certain moment, the interface suggests some products.
