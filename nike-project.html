Brief

For the Integrated Communication course, the project was to create a communication campaign for Nike during the Olimpics games 2016, in Rio de Janeiro.

Strategy

The idea behind our campaign was based on three keywords: Aspire, Inspire and Courage.

The campaign line that we figure out was CROSS THE LINE:

Cross the line will encourage you to go out outside your personal boundaries (lines) to reach your goal no matter how far or high it is. It plays on the negative connotation of "crossing the line" and putting it in a positive light.

We based our campaign in 4 main touchpoints: billboard posters, video commercial, Snapchat guerrilla and a parallel competition.
