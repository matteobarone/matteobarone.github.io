Brief

After several meetings and discussions, Samsung ask to the SPD students to provide a digital experience for their customers, that is able to attract new customers and engage them into Samsung World.

Concept

After a research about the brand and its products, we tought that the best way to engage customers and make them discover the vast Samsung world was through an arcade based app game.

The app can be used by the users everywhere simulating the usage of the products. It will entertain the users while teaching them something about the various technology advancement of Samsung products and create a connection with them, thatâ€™s why we named our app "Samsung Contact".

Workflow

The project workflow started from the begin with an analysis and a research of the market.

Then we came out with the concept and the customer journey.

The last part was focused on the design of the app and the prototype (developed with Invisionapp), that is also the output of our work.

We also developed one of the games with an high-fidelity prototype, the washing machine game, to show the features of it.
