Project goals

Review the Kiosk user interface in order to make it more usable.

Create a responsive web-app that permit to sell all their type of tickets.

Location

SPD Scuola Politecnica di Design - Milano

Team work with:
Diego Bernal
Paola Panzera
Sindhura Thulasi

Brief

ATM is the public transport company in Milan. They have two digital selling channel for their tickets:

- kiosks: automatic machine located in each metro station;

- online website.

They ask to review the Kiosk user interface and create a responsive web-app to increase their sell throw the digital channels.

Implementing the Kiosks UI process.

Our first part started with an analysis: we did some interviews, in two different way: traditional interview to customers, playing the role of the user. Based on this research we create three personas and related tasks flow.

With this information we developed a first prototype based on our research and results. Then we made a user testing, and afterwards we fixed bugs based on it.

Web-app process, “Mobile first” approach

After the Kiosks UI redesign, we focus our work on the web.

We based our design on the functionality, using few flat colors (according with ATM palette colors). The output was a prototype first in Invisionapp, then developed also in high-fidelity with HTML/CSS and responsive design.
