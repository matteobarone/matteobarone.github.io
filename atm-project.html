ATM

Team work with

Diego Bernal, Paola Panzera and Sindhura Jakkampury.

Description

ATM is the public transport company in Milan. They have two digital selling channel for their tickets:

  1. kiosks: automatic machine located in each metro station;
  2. online website.

The project main goals are:

  1. to review the Kiosk user interface in order to make it more usable;
  2. create a responsive web-app that permit to sell all their type of tickets.

Kiosks UI process:

  • Analysis: we did some interviews, in two different way: traditional interview to customers, playing the role of the user.
  • Personas and tasks flow.
  • First prototype based on our research and results.
  • User testing.
  • Fix bug based on the user testing session, and developing the final project.
  • Used tools: Balsaiq Mockup 3 for the prototype (https://balsamiq.com).

Web-app process, “Mobile first” approach:

  • Wireframe and Moodboard.
  • Design the UI.
  • Middle-fidelity prototype in Invision.
  • High-fidelity prototype in HTML and CSS (http://matteobarone.it/projects/atmprototype/).
