SILENZIO

Monica Armani | 2013

"Silenzio is a family of products that we have imagined as a result of our experience. Following the idea of enhancing the spaces we create an object that usually warm up the space and have a precise function.

The rest came of its own accord with the encounter of two outstanding companies in their respective technological areas: the Italian lighting manufacturer Luceplan and the Danish textile firm Kvadrat"

Monica Armani

Suspension lamps that behave like upholstered furnishings and, if required, can be customized with a wide range of fabrics. Besides developing performance for acoustic comfort, these lamps decorate spaces, personalizing them, becoming their protagonists and settings the mood.
